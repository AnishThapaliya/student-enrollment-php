Why kent?

At Kent, we take a holistic approach in the development of our students, which is why we provide services that are tailored to both their professional and personal growth. Kent caters to diverse student learning needs and aims to identify and respond to the learning needs of all students. Kent provides free and confidential counselling support. We'd love to see you to succeed in your studies, enjoy life, develop lifelong friends, and have fun while working towards your academic and career goals. Kent holds a range of extra-curricular events and activities for students all throughout the year. These events provide students with opportunities to create new friendships, develop skills, access support and receive recognition as part of the LEAD program.
